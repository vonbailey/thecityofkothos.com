Creston, Captian of the Guard

He is a man who has wandered to the far reaches of the Hcratian Empire and back, he manages to find himself cast into the political intrigues of Kothos while trying to make a living.  Consort to the Queen and hated by the second most powerful woman in Kothos, for a slight he isn't even aware of, sends his life into a turmoil of magic, assassinations and political power struggles of which he really didn't want to be bothered.
