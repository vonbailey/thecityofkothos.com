Princess Tuppa Tu

Princess Tuppa Tu, Second Born Daughter of Queen Balla Tu.  The Pricess was born during the floods of the seventh season of the Reign of Balla Tu.  The pricess was born with eyes of blue.  Because of this, many predicted her birth was an omen that the powers of Karpa were to be strong in the future.This had proved to be true when, soon after, Batuma Kan, First Lady of the Blue, became a very powerful priestess in the city and the new leader of the Blue Clan.  But the powers of Karpa also pulsed strong in the veins of the young princess.
